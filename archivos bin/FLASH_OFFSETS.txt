CYBERDECK MINI ESP32 - FLASH FILES

Web flasher / one-file flash:
0x00000000  CYBERDECK-MINI-ESP32-firmware-merged.bin

External tools / manual flash:
0x00000000  bootloader.bin
0x00008000  partitions.bin
0x0000E000  boot_app0.bin
0x00010000  firmware.bin

Notes:
- The three main files for external tools are bootloader.bin, partitions.bin and firmware.bin.
- boot_app0.bin is also included because Arduino ESP32-S3 builds normally flash it at 0xE000.
- Board target: ESP32-S3 DevKitC-1 N8 / 8MB flash.
